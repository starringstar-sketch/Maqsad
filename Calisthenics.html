Morning Routine (5:00 AM - 7:30 AM)

  • **Bird-Dog:** Strengthens core and glutes.
  • **Glute Bridges:** Activates and strengthens the glutes.

  • **Bodyweight Squats:** 3 sets of 15-20 reps.
  • **Walking Lunges:** 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg.
  • **Single-Leg RDLs:** 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
  • **Push-Ups:** 3 sets to failure.
  • **Inverted Rows:** 3 sets to failure.
  • **Plank:** 3 sets, hold for 60 seconds.
  • **Leg Raises:** 3 sets of 15 reps.

Stretching & Cool-Down (6:45 - 7:30)

  • **Standing Hip Flexor Stretch:** Hold for 30 seconds per leg.
  • **Pigeon Pose:** Hold for 30 seconds per leg.
  • **Lying Spinal Twist:** Hold for 30 seconds per side.
  • **Hamstring Stretch:** Hold for 30 seconds per leg.
  • **Quadriceps Stretch:** Hold for 30 seconds per leg.

Evening Routine (6:00 PM - 7:00 PM)

Warm-Up & Mobility (6:00 - 6:10)

  • **Light Cardio:** Jumping jacks or jogging in place for 5 minutes.
  • **Bodyweight Squats:** 10 repetitions.
  • **Leg Swings:** 10 swings in each direction per leg.

Core & Posterior Chain (6:10 - 6:40)

  • **Plank Variations:** Standard and side planks, 30-60 seconds each.
  • **Supermans:** 3 sets of 15 reps.
  • **Bird-Dog:** 3 sets of 10-15 reps per side.
  • **Reverse Crunches:** 3 sets of 15-20 reps.

Deep Stretching & Recovery (6:40 - 7:00)

  • **Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch:** Hold for 30-45 seconds per leg.
  • **Pigeon Pose:** Hold for 1 minute per leg.

  • **Seated Hamstring Stretch:** Hold for 30-45 seconds.
